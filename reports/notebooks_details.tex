\section{Analysis and Prototyping (notebooks)}
The \texttt{notebooks} directory contains the initial research phase of the project, focusing on Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).

\subsection{EDA Explorer}
The primary notebook, \texttt{eda\_explorer.ipynb}, provides:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Visual Sparsity Checks}: Heatmaps showing the distribution of missing values before and after enrichment.
    \item \textbf{Tactical Visualization}: Preliminary charts analyzing the evolution of ball possession and shot efficiency in the Champions League from 2011 to 2025.
\end{itemize}
These notebooks were instrumental in identifying the need for a multi-source enrichment pipeline.
